About us

Mount Everest Secondary Boarding School (MESBS), established in 1983 A.D. (2040 B.S.), is one of the most trusted and reputed academic institutions in Siraha District, Madhesh Province, Nepal. For over four decades, the school has been dedicated to providing quality education, discipline, and holistic development to students from early childhood to higher secondary level. Located at Siraha-7, MESBS continues its mission of shaping young minds with integrity, innovation, and commitment.

MESBS offers Montessori-based Pre-School (PG, Nursery, LKG, UKG), School Level (Class 1–10), and +2 programs in Management, Law, and Education under the National Examinations Board (NEB). MESBS ensures that every student achieves academic excellence, confidence, and essential life skills. The school provides a wide range of facilities including transportation, hostels for boys and girls, library, science and computer laboratories, audio-visual classrooms, seminar hall, and well-furnished state-of-the-art classrooms. Extra-curricular activities, sports, music, tours, and counseling services help students grow physically, mentally, emotionally, and socially.

MESBS is powered by a team of experienced, trained, and dedicated teachers who focus on discipline, moral values, creativity, communication skills, and leadership abilities. Through balanced academics and co-curricular activities, the school aims to develop responsible, confident, and capable citizens of the future. MESBS ensures that every student learns in a safe, student-friendly, and achievement-oriented environment.
